FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of practices do you work with?

We specialize in TMS therapy and Ketamine infusion practices, as well as general mental health and psychiatry clinics. Our services are tailored to help practices across the mental health spectrum grow and thrive.

How long does it take to see results?

Results vary depending on the services. For marketing initiatives, you can expect to see measurable improvements within 2-3 months. Practice development consulting often shows immediate operational improvements, with long-term growth building over 6-12 months.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes! We offer various levels of ongoing support from monthly retainers for marketing services to quarterly check-ins for practice development. We believe in building long-term partnerships with our clients.

What is the free clinic assessment?

Our free clinic assessment is a comprehensive 360-degree evaluation of your practice. We analyze your workflows, marketing, patient experience, and operational efficiency to identify opportunities for improvement and growth.
