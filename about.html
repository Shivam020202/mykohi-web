Our Story

A Movement Rooted
in Compassion

MyKohi was born from love. Specifically, the love and spirit of Kohi, Dr. Karan's beloved rescue pup whose grace and empathy touched every life she encountered.

Kohi reminded us of the simple, transformative power of compassion: that by serving others with presence and purpose, we can heal in ways beyond measure. Inspired by her legacy, MyKohi stands as more than a consulting or marketing agency.

MyKohi also honors the compassion, wisdom, and warmth embodied by Buddy, whose gentle nature and unwavering presence continue to inspire kindness, purpose, and strength within the MyKohi family.
